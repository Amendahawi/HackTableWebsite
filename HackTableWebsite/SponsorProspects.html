//Who we are?

We are a student run organization based in the Seattle area working to make STEM education more accessible and fun for everyone. Our goal is to create a platform where students can collaborate with others who share their interests in computer science and STEM. To do this, we will be hosting our second free hackathon, an event where computer programmers and others involved in software development will work together to develop a project. We plan to launch it in summer 2021.

//Why sponsor us?

Our first Hackathon was a huge success, and we had over 340 hackers participate from 6 continents competing for $37,500 in prizes. Judges from Microsoft, AT&T, Google, Southern Utah University, EazyML, Amazon, and Tesla STEM all selected winning apps in multiple categories. The event inspired many advanced and novice programmers to start or continue pursuing computer science, and we hope to build on this success. Help us inspire the next generation of computer scientists!
